Reach Out To Us Today

The Computer Science Department and the College of Science at Purdue University support a unique program called Reach Out for Computer Science (ROCS).
ROCS is made up of computer science students with a passion for giving back to their community, particularly when it involves teaching about technology.
The group works with students from kindergarten to 12th grade in the state of Indiana and surrounding areas. In addition to doing information sessions, hands-on activities, and full computer science lessons, the ROCS group also is committed to helping develop new and exciting ways for students to get involved in the world of technology.
